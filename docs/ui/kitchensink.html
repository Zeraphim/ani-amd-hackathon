06 — Motion principles

Motion means growth

Every animation ties back to one idea: things that are alive move. Reveals rise like sprouts, numbers count up like a harvest tally, gradients ripen, and the node-network in the hero breathes.

Reveal on scroll

Content rises 26px and fades in with a staggered delay. Uses IntersectionObserver; respects prefers-reduced-motion.

Magnetic + spring

Buttons drift toward the cursor and spring back on release. Playful, tactile, never sluggish.

Living gradient

The harvest gradient slowly shifts position, so freshness meters and hero text feel warm and alive rather than static.

Accessibility: all motion is disabled automatically when the user prefers reduced motion. Color contrast follows the brand book — white/cream on green and black on cream both clear WCAG AA.
